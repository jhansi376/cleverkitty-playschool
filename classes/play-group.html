Tiny Steps,
Big Discoveries!

At CleverKitty, we understand that the first school experience is very important for every child. Our Playgroup curriculum is carefully designed to help children feel safe, happy, and comfortable before starting structured learning.

Settling & Observation

First Few Weeks

During the initial weeks, our main focus is helping children feel safe and comfortable in their new environment.

🎯

Our Focus

  • Helping children feel comfortable in the classroom environment
  • Allowing them to freely explore and interact
  • Encouraging bonding with the class teacher
  • Observing their behavior, communication, and response
  • Helping them get habituated to routines and simple instructions
👀

We Gently Observe

  • Do they feel secure in the classroom atmosphere?
  • Do they respond to simple commands?
  • Are they participating in group activities?

✨ Only after the child is well-settled and confident, we gradually introduce structured learning.

✏️
