About Us

A Journey of
Nurturing Minds

Founded in 2019, CleverKitty Daycare and Play School was created with a simple vision — to provide a safe, happy, and activity-based learning space for young children.

What began with just 10 little learners and a big dream has now grown into a trusted institution with 100+ happy students and satisfied parents by 2026.

At CleverKitty, we believe every child is unique. We create a joyful environment where children learn through play, creativity, communication, and real-world experiences.

Principal’s Desk ✉️

"Dear Parents, Welcome to CleverKitty Daycare and Play School!"

Since our establishment in 2019, our mission has been to provide quality early education in a safe and nurturing environment. Starting with just 10 students, we have grown to serve over 100 happy learners by 2026.

At CleverKitty, we focus not only on academics but also on communication skills, creativity, social awareness, and emotional development. Through engaging activities, celebrations, and real-world exposure, we build a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

We thank all our parents for their trust and continued support. Together, we shape confident and happy children ready for the future.
