Founded with Love

Started with a Simple Dream

Ten years ago, we looked for a playschool that felt less like a classroom and more like a living room. We wanted a place where labels didn't define kids, where laughter was the primary language, and every scribble was a celebrated masterpiece.

CleverKitty was born from that vision—to provide a magical sanctuary where safety meets curiosity, and every child is loved like family.

🖋️

Sarah Jenkins

Founder & Director

The CleverKitty Promise

Emotional Safety

We prioritize emotional well-being as much as physical safety. Every child has a primary caregiver for ultimate comfort.

Nature Learning

Daily garden time! We learn from bugs, leaves, and mud. Connecting with nature grounds our little ones' spirits.

Joyful Discovery

No rote learning. We use music, art, and play to teach concepts. If they aren't smiling, we aren't doing it right!
