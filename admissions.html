A Second Home for Your Little One

Heart-to-Heart Questions

Q. My child has never been away from me. Will they cry?

Some tears are normal at first! It shows how much they love you. We are experts at "separation anxiety." We use extra cuddles, distraction play, and we keep you updated with photos so you know the moment they start smiling.

Q. What about food and potty training?

Don't worry! We help with potty training when they are ready, with zero pressure. For food, we encourage healthy eating in a group setting - you'd be surprised how kids eat veggies when their friends are doing it!

Q. Is my child safe while exploring?

Safety is our top priority. We have child-proofed corners, high-quality flooring, and a very high teacher-to-child ratio. We let them explore within a secure, supervised "circle of safety."
